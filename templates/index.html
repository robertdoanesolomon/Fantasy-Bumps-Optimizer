Fantasy Bumps — optimizer

Choose Summer Eights or Torpids, set the year (or tick Latest bumps), then Load crews to pull market data from fantasybumps.org.uk. Select boats, use overbump where available, set your crab budgets for men and women, and Run optimization for the top 10 combinations per side, with popularity tables alongside. Payouts match the site; prices use live buy buttons when the market is open, or estimated costs after the event ends. Latest bumps scans downward from the max year until it finds a live men’s market page for that series—so when the site adds eights2026 or torpids2026, the next load picks it automatically (no redeploy), until we pass the configured year range.
